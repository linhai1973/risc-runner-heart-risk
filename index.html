欢迎使用心脏风险评估系统 Welcome to Heart Risk Assessment System

本系统通过自适应问卷评估您的心脏风险指数（0-100分），并提供个性化健康建议。This system evaluates your heart risk index (0-100) through an adaptive questionnaire and provides personalized health recommendations.

评估内容 Assessment Contents

  • 年龄与基础健康状况 Age and basic health status
  • 心血管疾病家族史 Family history of cardiovascular disease
  • 近期身体症状表现 Recent physical symptoms
  • 运动习惯与强度 Exercise habits and intensity
  • 急救知识掌握程度 First aid knowledge level

⚠️ 本评估仅供参考，不能替代专业医疗诊断This assessment is for reference only and cannot replace professional medical diagnosis
